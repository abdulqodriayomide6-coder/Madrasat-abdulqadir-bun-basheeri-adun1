About Us

About Our Institution

Madrasat Ash-Sheikh Abdul Qadir Bin Bushairi Adun is a dedicated Islamic educational institute committed to delivering authentic, structured Islamic education to students worldwide. We bridge the gap between traditional Madrasah scholarship and the convenience of modern technology.

Located in Ajibesin, Ogidi, Ilorin, Kwara State, Nigeria, we serve the Muslim community with dedication and excellence, striving to be a beacon of Islamic knowledge and values.

Authenticity Discipline Sincerity Excellence Accessibility

"Our mission is to revive authentic Islamic knowledge through structured and accessible learning, nurturing students who live by the Qur'an."

— Sheikh Abdul Qadir Bin Bushairi · Founder
